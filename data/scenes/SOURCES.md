# Image sources

The sample frames in this folder are from the **"Packages" object-detection dataset** by
Joseph Nelson on Roboflow Universe, released into the **public domain (CC0 1.0)**.

- Dataset: https://universe.roboflow.com/joseph-nelson/packages-g0ton
- Public mirror: https://public.roboflow.com/object-detection/packages-dataset
- License: **CC0 1.0 (Public Domain)** — no attribution required; provenance noted here as a courtesy.
- Accessed: 2026-07-02.

**Processing:** the originals (4032×3024) were cropped to 16:10 (favoring the lower portion,
where the packages sit) and resized to 1280×800 for the demo frame. No other edits.
